About Quarantine

The quarantine feature isolates potentially malicious files to prevent them from affecting your system. Files in quarantine are stored securely and cannot execute or cause harm.

Important: If you believe a file was incorrectly identified as malicious, you can restore it to its original location or a new location of your choice.
Warning: Restoring a truly malicious file could pose a security risk to your system. Only restore files if you are confident they are safe.
