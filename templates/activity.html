About Activity Tracking

SmartFileGuardian keeps a record of your security-related activities to help you monitor and manage your system's security. This includes:

  • File Scans: Records of files you've scanned and their results
  • Quarantine Actions: When files are moved to quarantine
  • Restore Actions: When files are restored from quarantine
  • Delete Actions: When quarantined files are permanently deleted
Note: Activity history is stored locally on your device and is not shared with any external servers.
